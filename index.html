Hello! My name is Rae Schneider

I am a software developer and librarian based in Denver, CO. I have over 10 years of experience working in both public and academic libraries. I specialize in library technology, systems integrations, and web development. I am passionate about delivering high quality digital services to diverse user groups.

If you are looking for a software developer that understands the needs of libraries and the importance of providing a high quality digital experience to your patrons, I would love to hear from you!

Please take a look at my portfolio and feel free to contact me about your next project.

About me

I have a Master's degree in Library and Information Science from the University of Washington. I am passionate about providing access to information to the community. I have held a variety of technical roles both inside and outside the library sector.

I have worked with teams of software developers in an agile environment. I have experience managing projects and working with customers to implement software to their specifications. In addition, I have experience in QA, software testing, and providing application support.
